Viet Puzzle

How to Play Vietnam Puzzle

  • Players guess a Vietnamese Province in 6 guesses
  • Each guess is a valid province in Vietnam
  • After each guess, player will know distance and direction from the player's choice to the target location.

Example:

Lạng Sơn
698.73 km

Your guess Lạng Sơn is 698.73 km away from the target location, the target location is in the South direction. Let try again!

Khánh Hòa
394 km

Your guess Khánh Hòa is 394 km away from the target location, the target location is in the North direction. Let try again!

Quảng Nam
0km

Your guess is excellent answer, great job. Congratulation!
